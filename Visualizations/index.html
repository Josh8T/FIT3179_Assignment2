An Analytical View into Unicorn Startups

Defined as privately held startups valued at $1 billion or more, unicorns are recognized for their substantial impact on the industries they operate within and their notable investment traction. Utilizing Vega-Lite visualizations, this platform offers you a detailed analysis, enabling an examination of patterns, trends, and insights into the world where business, innovation, and significant capital coalesce.

Choropleth Number of Unicorns per Country & Growth of Unicorns Globally

The choropleth map gives an overview of the number of unicorn startups by country. The area chart tracks the growth of unicorn startup companies globally. Using the line chart provided, you can isolate specific range of time to view for more details. This will provide a visual representation of the economic impact of these startups on different countries.

Distribution of Unicorn Industries

This pie chart depicts the distribution of unicorn startups across various industries. Each slice represents a distinct sector, with the accompanying number indicating the total unicorn companies in that domain. Notably, the Media & Entertainment industry leads with a staggering 370 startups, showcasing its dominant presence in the unicorn ecosystem.

This bar chart showcases the top-tier unicorn startups based on their astounding valuations. Leading the pack is ByteDance, reaching valuations soaring above $200 billion. Use the interactive feature below to adjust the number of companies displayed, always ranked by their valuation prowess.

This scatterplot to explore how countries stack up in terms of unicorn company count and combined valuation. Notably, the United States and China stand out as the titans of the startup world. Utilize the 'Continent' filter to narrow down your view and uncover regional trends.
